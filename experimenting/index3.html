About Me

Tyler Mitchell is an Arizona native and an ASU alumn. Following his graduation from ASU in 2011, Tyler began pursuing a career in consumer banking and finance with Wells Fargo Bank. After a few years of exemplary service, Tyler worked his way into a promotion as a loan underwriter with Wells Fargo Dealer Services. Most recenly, Tyler worked as an Underwriter for Flagship Credit Acceptance.

After six years in the finance industry, Tyler has decided to pursue a career as a Web Developer, an industry that has always appealed to him. To greatly improve his coding skill set, Tyler is partaking in the University of Arizona's full stack web development coding bootcamp. He will learn skills with Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, Java, MongoDB and MySQL, MERN and several other technologies.

Be sure to check out Tyler's GitHub page to see his working projects!
